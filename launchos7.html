The four ways small businesses get burned.

We talked to 312 local business owners — dentists, medspas, plumbers, electricians, coaches. The same four traps showed up over and over. Maybe one of them is why you're here.

01

The retainer trap.

Agencies need 90 days to "optimize." That's 90 days of $3,000–$5,000 invoices. When results don't come, they blame your business model — and keep billing.

£4,000 was my life's savings and I really thought it was going to work. I just feel like I was sold a dream.

02

The software graveyard.

You buy GoHighLevel or HubSpot because everyone says you need a CRM. Two weeks later, the "blank canvas" is still blank and you're paying $97/mo for nothing.

I'm new to HubSpot and there are sO MUCH OPTIONS, it's overwhelming. This isn't what I'm good at.

03

The tire-kicker lead trap.

Angi, HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack — same lead, sold to 5–7 of your competitors. You drive 40 minutes for a free estimate. The "lead" already hired someone else.

Angi is almost always a waste of money. They sell the same lead to multiple contractors. A lot of them are fake or tire-kickers.

The freelancer ghost.

50% deposit upfront. Two weeks of silence. A broken site. A bizarre excuse. And then — nothing. You're back at square one with less money than you started.

Honest answers · 09

Things you'd ask before paying anyone.

These are the exact objections we hear on every call. We'd rather answer them once, in writing, than dance around them in a sales pitch.

What happens to my money if you don't deliver in 5 days?
Full refund. Same day. No "let me check with my manager." If we don't hand you a working, tested booking machine by the end of business on day five, the first month's $349 is returned to your card immediately — and we keep building until it's done, on us. It happens roughly 4% of the time. We honor it every time.
Do I have to learn HubSpot, GoHighLevel, or any other software?
No. That's the whole point. Your operator handles every dashboard, every automation, every API connection, every weird settings page. You get a 15-minute walkthrough video on day five showing you how to view new bookings on your phone — that's it. If you ever want to learn the back-end, we'll teach you. If you don't, you never have to log in.
Are the leads exclusive to me, or shared like Angi?
They're yours. Period. Every lead is captured directly on infrastructure you own — your domain, your CRM, your phone number, your Google Business Profile. We're not a lead marketplace and we never resell anything. If you cancel tomorrow, the leads keep flowing to your account because the system is yours.
Is there a setup fee, deposit, or annual contract?
None. $349 is the entire bill. There's no $2,000 onboarding fee, no $5,000 build fee buried somewhere, no annual contract, no auto-escalating price. You pay $349 on day one, we build for five days, and you pay $349 each month after to keep us on retainer for maintenance and small edits. Pause anytime. Cancel anytime.
What if I want to pause during a slow month?
One click in your dashboard pauses billing for up to 90 days at a stretch. Your infrastructure stays live, leads keep flowing in, automations keep firing — we just don't take your money during the pause. When you're ready to come back, one click resumes everything.
Who do you build for? Will this work for my business?
Local service businesses with a clear offer: medspas, dentists, chiropractors, plumbers, HVAC, electricians, contractors, coaches, lawyers, accountants, home services of all kinds. If you have a phone, a calendar, and a service people pay for — yes. If you're an e-commerce store, a SaaS company, or an enterprise B2B operation, we'll politely turn you away on the fit call. No hard feelings.
What if the operator I get isn't a good fit?
Tell us. We'll swap them same-week. Every operator has shipped at least 20 builds and carries a 90%+ retention rate, but personality matters too. There's zero awkwardness in asking — it's built into how we work.
