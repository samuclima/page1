Frequently
asked
questions.

The objections, in order. If you've been burned by an agency or ghosted by a freelancer, you'll find your worry here.

Is this just another website builder?
No — and please don't confuse us with one. Website builders give you a blank canvas and a steep learning curve. We're the people who actually build the thing. You never touch a drag-and-drop editor, fight with plugins, or watch a 20-minute tutorial. Our operator builds your full system — pages, CRM, automations, booking — and hands you the keys.
How is this different from GoHighLevel or HubSpot?
GoHighLevel and HubSpot sell software. We sell a finished system. You can buy GHL for $97/mo — but then you spend the next two months learning it, configuring it, and wondering why your automations aren't firing. We use proven infrastructure under the hood and do all the implementation for you. You're not buying a tool. You're buying the system already running on it.
What if I cancel — do I lose my website and contacts?
No. Your contacts, leads, content, brand assets, and business data belong to you — period. Export your CRM to CSV with one click whenever you want. The subscription pays for the service, build, and ongoing support. We don't hold your data hostage.
$349/mo seems expensive just for software.
That's because it isn't software. It's a productized growth team. Compare it to hiring a freelancer ($3–8k one-time, then they ghost), an agency ($5–15k setup + $2–5k/mo retainer), or a marketing hire ($60k+/year). $349/mo is what a part-time admin makes in two days. Except this one ships a working system in five days and never quits.
How fast is "2–5 business days," really?
Really. Once you finish onboarding (about 15 minutes), your operator starts building. Most systems are live by day three. Day five is the outside edge for more complex builds. It's fast because we don't reinvent the wheel — we use battle-tested architectures and skip the six weeks of agency "discovery." If we don't deliver in 7 days, your first month is refunded.
Who's actually doing the work? Is this outsourced?
Real LaunchOS operators — vetted specialists trained on our system stack. You'll know your operator by name and have a direct line to them. Every build runs through a strict QA checklist before launch. No drop-servicing, no anonymous handoffs.
Will this work for my specific industry?
Probably — we've built systems for medspas, contractors, coaches, course creators, agencies, dentists, and ecommerce brands. The mechanics of lead capture, follow-up, and booking are universal. Your operator tailors the messaging, offer, and workflow to your specific business during onboarding.
What if I already have a website?
Keep it. We can attach our funnels, CRM, and booking system to your existing domain — or run the lead system on a subdomain (like offers.yourbusiness.com) without touching your main site. If your current site is underperforming, we'll rebuild it as part of the system.
Are there hidden fees?
No setup fees. No hourly billing. No surprise invoices. The subscription covers the build, the system, hosting, and ongoing implementation. The only at-cost extras are SMS/email send volumes if you go big — passed through at provider cost, not marked up.
What if I want changes after launch?
Two options. (1) You have full dashboard access and can edit anything yourself if you prefer. (2) Just message your operator — small tweaks and ongoing updates are included in your subscription. Same as having a webmaster on retainer, except we actually answer.
